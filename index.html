About the company

Who we are and how we operate

DemID Int.DOO is an international company with roots tracing back to 2006 in Germany. Since our founding, we have focused on the supply of industrial raw materials, building a reputation as a reliable and trusted partner.

Today, our headquarters are in Belgrade, Serbia, with offices and representatives across Europe and Asia. We are a truly international team, united by deep market insight and a passion for logistics and trade.

We specialize in the supply of non-ferrous metals, ferroalloys, specialty chemicals, and technology metals. Our team consists of experienced professionals with many years of expertise in the metals and mining industry — combining global perspective with local knowledge.

We have built a strong network of partners across Europe and Asia. Today, our products are shipped to Uzbekistan, Serbia, Kazakhstan, India, and key countries within the European Union — and we are actively expanding into new markets.

Our approach is simple: reliable logistics, transparent pricing, and consistent quality. We know that in this industry, trust is everything. That's why every order, from the first inquiry to final delivery, is handled with precision and care.

At DemID Int.DOO, we don't just trade — we build long-term partnerships based on integrity, expertise, and results.

Our products

What we offer

Non-ferrous metals

We offer tin, zinc, and lead — always in stock, ready for dispatch. We work daily with all types of non-ferrous metals, ensuring reliable and flexible supply.

Ferroalloys

We supply high-quality ferroalloys: ferrotungsten, ferrosilicon, ferromanganese, ferrochrome, etc. Stable volumes, consistent quality, and on‑time delivery.

Specialty Chemicals

We offer copper sulfate, zinc sulfate, and other specialty chemicals for industrial and agricultural applications.

Technology & Rare Earth Metals

We supply antimony, indium, chromium, bismuth, arsenic, and other critical metals for high-tech and industrial applications.

Copper Products

We offer copper wire and copper powder — high‑purity materials suitable for industrial manufacturing and electrical applications.

Chemical Raw Materials

We supply chemical raw materials for light and heavy industry, ensuring consistent quality and reliable supply for diverse manufacturing processes.
