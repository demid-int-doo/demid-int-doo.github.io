About the company

Who we are and how we operate

DemID Int.DOO is a Serbian company specializing in the supply of non-ferrous metals and ferroalloys. Founded in 2025, we are a young, dynamic team with a clear mission — to deliver high-quality raw materials to markets that matter.

In a short time, we have built a strong network of partners across Europe and Asia. Today, our metals are shipped to Uzbekistan, Serbia, Kazakhstan, and India — and we are actively expanding into new markets.

Our approach is simple: reliable logistics, transparent pricing, and consistent quality. We know that in the metals industry, trust is everything. That's why every order, from the first inquiry to final delivery, is handled with precision and care.

At DemID Int.DOO, we don't just trade metals — we build long-term partnerships based on integrity and results.
