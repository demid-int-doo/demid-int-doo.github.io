About the company

Who we are and how we operate

DemID Int.DOO is a Serbian company specializing in the supply of non-ferrous metals and ferroalloys. Our team consists of experienced professionals with many years of expertise in the metals and mining industry. We combine deep market knowledge with a dynamic, client-focused approach to deliver exceptional value.

We have built a strong network of partners across Europe and Asia. Today, our metals are shipped to Uzbekistan, Serbia, Kazakhstan, India, and key countries within the European Union — and we are actively expanding into new markets.

Our approach is simple: reliable logistics, transparent pricing, and consistent quality. We know that in the metals industry, trust is everything. That's why every order, from the first inquiry to final delivery, is handled with precision and care.

At DemID Int.DOO, we don't just trade metals — we build long-term partnerships based on integrity and results.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, reliability, and trust in every shipment.

Our products

What we offer

Non-ferrous metals

We offer wholesale supplies of lead, zinc, and tin. Upon request, we can arrange delivery of other types of non-ferrous metals.

Ferroalloys

We supply high-quality ferroalloys for the metallurgical industry: ferrosilicon, ferromanganese, ferrochrome. We ensure stable volumes and consistent quality.
